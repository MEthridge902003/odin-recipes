World's Best Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is a classic comforting dish that takes a little extra time but is worth the extra effort for an amazing meal

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large pot cook the sausage and ground over medium heat until evenly brown. Drain excess grease
  2. Stir in the onion and garlic. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant
  3. Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste.Season with salt and pepper. Simmer 1 1/2 hours stirring occasionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and set aside
  5. In a bowl mix together ricotta cheese and egg
  6. Preheat oven to 375
  7. To assemble, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.Layer with noodle,then ricotta mixture, then mozarella slices, then parmesan
  8. Repeat layers until ingredients are used, ending with sauce and cheeses
  9. Bake for 25 minutes. Let stand 15 minutes before serving
