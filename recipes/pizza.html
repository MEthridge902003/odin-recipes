Easy Homemade Pizza

Description

This recipe uses pre-made dough for a fast and delicious weeknight pizza. It's fully customizable with your favorite toppings!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pre-made pizza dough or fresh dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushroom, ect )
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon italian seasonings (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450
  2. On a lightly floured surface, stretch or roll out pizza dough into a 12 inch circle or rectangle
  3. Brush the edges of dough with olive oil
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border for the crust
  5. Spinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce
  6. Add your desired topping and desired optional seasonings
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly
  8. Let the pizza cool for 5 minutes before slicing and serving
